know ur OP

 

 

 

Pretty much just know:

 

The impersonal se: which means "people in general". Conjugate the verb into the Usted or Ustedes form depending on if it is plural or not, and add a "se" in front of it

 

The negative Tu command: when you need to say "no dont do this" then conjugate the verb in question into the yo form, then change the ending into the opposite chart's conjugation. So, for example if you want to say "don't chop your hand" it would be "no pices tu mano"
