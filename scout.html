What is a sweet spot?

Sweet spots are a mechanic that make the damage dealt by Scout rifles start and end low, with a one hit kill range in the middle.

 

 

 

This means that there are several guns you should be using for each given range:

 

Close- Mid (30-80m)

SMLE mkIII, Martini-Henry, Gewehr M.95

 

 

Mid-Long (60-100m)

Rusian 1895, Gewehr 98

 

 

Long (100m+)

Gewehr 98/ m1903 Springfield

 

 

Any of the other gun not listed here do not have the sweet spot mechanic.
